Software Corner

About


“If debugging is the process of removing software bugs, then programming must be the process of putting them in.” - Edsger Dijkstra

We invite all programming enthusiasts to participate in online programming contest of IIIT Gwalior. IOPC is the annual International Online Programming Contest of IIIT Gwalior, organized by the Infotsav team. It is a prestigious programming contest where each coder whacks his brain to code solutions to the given problems. It is the place where optimization and proficiency matters, skills matter, hard work matters, trying till the very end matters and winning loads of cash prizes matter!

There is no need for abstract submissions for Software Corner competitions (even if you receive a mail saying so)

This is an online competition. No need to pay the Hospitality fees.

There is no need to worry if you are team status is "pending"



Event Structure

There will be only one phase in the contest and contain numerous questions.
All questions will be online. Each will be of different score points based on their difficulties.
The winners will be decided on the total score points.
Date and Time: March 1, 2020 (8 PM - 11 PM)



Guidelines

  1. IOPC is an individual rated contest this time. So, make registrations only individual.
  2. Students (undergraduates or graduates) or professionals across the world are eligible to participate.
  3. The contest will be hosted on hackerearth. In order to claim the prize money, you need to register on both platform and Infotsav website.
  4. Only participants who are currently registered as students in a university will be eligible for prizes.
  5. Each problem will have a certain specified input and output format. This format has to be adhered to, else the submission will not be accepted.
  6. Solutions can be submitted in any order.
  7. Any participant found to be indulging in any form of malpractice/plagiarism will be immediately disqualified by the Infotsav, IIIT Gwalior.

NOTE: Participants must follow the guidelines strictly. In Case of any disputes, the final decision will be in the hand of Infotsav Team, IIIT Gwalior.
