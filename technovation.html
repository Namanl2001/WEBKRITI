Event Structure

The event consists of 2 rounds: The Prelims and the Finals

Prelims: Each team will have to mail the abstract in the provided format together with pictures & videos explaining their project.
Shortlisted teams will Proceed to Finals.
Finals: There will be 2 stages in finals
Stage-1: You have to present your project in an exhibition at IIIT Gwalior in Infotsav’20, where each team will be given a space to arrange their projects. This will be reviewed by our judging panel and some questioning related to the project will be done. Teams will be selected for the Stage-2 based on their performance in Stage-1
Stage-2: It will be a standard presentation round where the finalists will have to present their idea to our eminent judging panel which will consist of entrepreneurs, industrialists, VCs and HNIs during Infotsav'20 within the allotted time (i.e. 8 mins ppt + 2 mins video) followed by the question-answer round (i.e. 5 mins).



Guidelines

  1. A maximum of 4 members per team are allowed.
  2. Participation is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students.
  3. Students from different colleges can also be a part of the same team.
  4. Shortlisted participants have to confirm their availability failing which they will be removed from the list of participating teams and the teams that have been kept on the waiting list will be called forth to participate.
  5. Certificates will be given to every member of the team who is present during the event. The event will span over 2 days. Presence of every member of the team is mandatory on both the days, failing to do so will lead to the team being disqualified.
  6. Winning teams of the last 3 years cannot participate with the same projects as they had last time. However, if they have a new idea no such restriction holds.
  7. All entrants are regarded as having a full understanding of the regulations for the competition and being willing to ­comply with every rule.
  8. The decision of the judges and the event managers will be final and binding.
  9. This year weightage is also given for the most entertaining entry, special prizes for it too.
  10. Apart from the best model a special jury award for the best presentation.
