Gamiacs






Think you have what it takes? Infotsav presents Gamiacs events where you can showcase you skills in games like CS-GO and PUBG.

About

For the First Time, Infotsav Presents Pubg Mobile Pan Mania Competitions for Participants. Pubg Mobile is one of the most played mobile games in the world. In which , esports is growing exponentially whether it is PMCO, PEL or Pro league. Infotsav'20 gives you opportunity to showcase your skills in mobile esports and win exciting Prizes. Form Teams with your Friends and get that coveted Chicken Dinner.


Event Structure

  1. Group Qualifiers

    We will make groups consisting of 20 Teams . This will be qualifiers round consisting of only one match(TPP-Erangel) . 4 teams with highest Points will be selected into next Round.

  2. Semifinals Rounds

    We will make 2 groups consisting of 20 Teams . This will be Semifinal Round consisting of 2(TPP-Erangel and TPP-Miramar) matches. Top 10 teams from each round will be selected for Final Event.

  3. Final Event

    This Final event will be of 3 Matches(TPP-Erangel, TPP-Miramar, TPP-Sanhok).

Rules

  1. You need to register in Infotsav Website for Claiming Prizes.

  3. Only registered players are allowed to play.
  4. We won't be responsible if anyone faces any glitch or internet issues during matches.
  5. If we find anyone using hacks, that team will be disqualified from the event.
  6. Every player should have all four maps downloaded , otherwise you will be kicked automatically.
